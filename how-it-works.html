Multi-Chain
EVM identity.
Solana when needed.

One EVM wallet handles your identity, vaults, and KYC. A Solana wallet is only added when a pool runs on Solana.

Wallet model

Your EVM wallet is your identity — used for vaulting, points, rank, KYC, and all EVM pools. A Solana wallet is optional, added only when you participate in a Solana pool.

Pool-level behavior

Every pool card shows its chain. CTAs reflect your actual status — invested, not invested, or missing a required wallet. If something blocks you, the button says what it is.

If a pool sells out and you didn't invest: the CTA becomes a chain-aware DEX link. Solana pools route to Meteora. EVM pools route to PancakeSwap or Uniswap.
Project Onboarding
Founder workflow.

Seven steps from draft to live. Steps 1–3 are self-serve and can be saved and returned to at any time. Steps 4–5 are under Blockstreet review. Steps 6–7 are deployment and go-live.

Project Basics

Name, ticker, target chain, category, website, social links, and team bios. Saved as Draft — edit freely at any time.

Token Economics

Total supply, tokens for sale, hard cap, soft cap, price, vesting schedule, TGE percentage, and cliff periods.

Pool Configuration

Phase 1 and Phase 2 timing, min/max contribution, fee structure, and target raise amount.

KYB / Due Diligence

Entity verification, team identity check, and smart contract audit status. Audit partner: Halborn.

Blockstreet Review

Internal compliance and quality check. Revisions send the founder back to step 3 — basics and tokenomics are preserved.

Contract Deployment

On-chain pool contract, token address verification, and platform fees settled.

Status transitions: Approved → Pre-Launch.

Pre-Launch → Live

Project appears as Scheduled. Investors can set notifications. Phase 1 opens automatically at the configured time.

Fee Structure
Transparent fees.
Paid by the project.

Investors pay nothing to participate.

Platform Fee

7.5% of raise

Charged on stablecoins raised. Paid by the project, not the investor.

Token Allocation

5% of project tokens

Negotiated per project. Applied toward marketing, liquidity provision, and KOL introductions.

BLOCK is a staking and access token — not an investment currency. Stablecoins are used for investment. No buy/sell tax on BLOCK. The only penalty is early unstaking.
